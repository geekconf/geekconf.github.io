µInstructor

Sessions are presented by Jeff Nickoloff, founding member of All in Geek Consulting Services, LLC.

    µService Owner
    Owned and engineered over 300 microservices with Amazon.com. A mix of platform and product services, they had a blended internal/external customer base. Jeff oversaw the deprecation of multiple decade old services, and the migration of hundreds of internal clients.
    µService Team Manager
    Nearly two years of his time at Amazon.com was spent in a leadership role. A significant component of that job is interfacing with service customers, peer teams, and leadership from other parts of the company. In this role Jeff witnessed how microservices play a major role in shaping those interactions and aligning interests.
    µService Author
    Jeff is writing Docker in Action. It is a book about Docker, a technology family that is changing the way we think about microservices and drasticly reducing learning curves and cost to market entry. His Linux container knowledge is broad and deep, but he has been a heavy virtual machine and cloud user since 2005.
    µService Software R&D
    General Experience
    He has 13 years of software engineering experience, and an engineering bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Arizona State University's Fulton School of Engineering. He is a tech polygot with experience in Java, Golang, JavaScript, NodeJS, C, Python, PHP, and Ruby on Rails.
